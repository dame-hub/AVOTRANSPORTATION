Fleet Vehicle Detailing Services Agreement

Scheduled, on-site fleet detailing for 20 to 80 vehicles — tiered from light reconditioning to full deep-clean detail. Governed by the laws of the State of Colorado.

Parties
This Fleet Detailing Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of   ("Effective Date") by and between AVO Transportation LLC, a Colorado limited liability company, Aurora, Colorado ("AVO" or "Provider"), and   ("Client"), located at  , Colorado. AVO and Client are each a "Party" and together the "Parties."

1. Scope of Services

1.1  AVO will provide professional vehicle detailing services (the "Services") for Client's fleet of between twenty (20) and eighty (80) vehicles (each a "Vehicle"), at the service tiers, vehicle classes, frequency, and prices set out in Exhibit A (Pricing Schedule) and to the standards in Exhibit B (Scope of Work).

1.2  Service tiers. Client may assign each Vehicle to one of three tiers: Tier 1 — Maintenance / Light Reconditioning, Tier 2 — Standard Full Detail, or Tier 3 — Deep Clean / Reconditioning, as defined in Exhibit B. Tiers may differ by Vehicle and may be changed with 48 hours' notice.

1.3  Location. Services are performed on-site at Client's yard or facility within the Denver metro service area unless otherwise agreed. Client provides reasonable access to water and 110V power where required, or AVO supplies self-contained equipment for an agreed surcharge per Exhibit A.

1.4  Schedule. Services are performed on a recurring basis (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) per Exhibit A, on mutually agreed days and time windows, including overnight/before-route windows where Client requires Vehicles ready by a set time.

2. Term & Renewal

2.1  This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of   months (the "Initial Term"). It then renews automatically for successive one-month periods unless either Party gives thirty (30) days' written notice of non-renewal.

3. Pricing, Volume Tiers & Taxes

3.1  Prices are the per-Vehicle rates in Exhibit A, adjusted by the applicable fleet volume discount (based on the number of Vehicles serviced per cycle) and any recurring-frequency credit, as shown in Exhibit A.

3.2  Minimum. Each visit is subject to a minimum charge equal to 20 Vehicles at the contracted tier, regardless of the number presented, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3.3  Add-ons & surcharges (e.g., excessive pet hair, biohazard, heavy mud/oxidation, engine bay, odor/ozone treatment, oversize vehicles) are billed per Exhibit A and disclosed before work begins.

3.4  Price-beat guarantee. AVO will beat any qualifying written quote from a licensed, insured Denver-metro competitor for a comparable fleet scope by 5%, presented before signing, consistent with AVO's published Price-Beat Policy.

3.5  Taxes. Prices exclude applicable Colorado state and local sales/use taxes, which are added where required. Each Party is responsible for its own income taxes.

3.6  Price changes. Rates are fixed for the Initial Term. AVO may adjust rates on 30 days' written notice for renewal periods, or sooner if Client's fleet size, vehicle condition, or scope materially changes.

4. Invoicing & Payment

4.1  AVO invoices   (weekly / bi-weekly / monthly). Payment is due net fifteen (15) days from the invoice date.

4.2  Late amounts accrue interest at 1.5% per month (or the maximum allowed by Colorado law, if lower). AVO may suspend Services on accounts more than 15 days past due after written notice.

4.3  Accepted payment: ACH/bank transfer, business check, or card. Card payments may carry a processing surcharge as permitted by law.

5. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide safe, lawful access to Vehicles and keys/fobs during the service window, and a reasonable work area.
  • Remove or secure cash, electronics, weapons, personal valuables, and proprietary equipment from Vehicles before service. AVO is not liable for unsecured items.
  • Disclose Vehicles with biohazards, hazardous materials, or pre-existing damage in advance.
  • Identify each Vehicle's assigned tier and any add-ons before each visit.

6. Quality Standard & Re-Do Warranty

6.1  AVO will perform the Services in a professional, workmanlike manner consistent with Exhibit B. If Client identifies a Vehicle that does not meet the contracted tier standard and notifies AVO within 24 hours of service, AVO will re-clean that Vehicle at no charge. This re-do remedy is Client's exclusive remedy for service-quality issues.

7. Insurance

7.1  AVO maintains, at its expense: Commercial General Liability of at least $1,000,000 per occurrence; Garage Keepers / care-custody-and-control coverage for Vehicles in AVO's care; Commercial Auto; and Workers' Compensation as required by Colorado law. AVO will furnish a Certificate of Insurance on request and can name Client as additional insured.

8. Limitation of Liability & Indemnity

8.1  Pre-existing conditions. AVO is not responsible for pre-existing damage, worn/failing components, aftermarket modifications, or damage revealed (not caused) by cleaning. AVO will document notable pre-existing damage before service where practical.

8.2  Cap. Except for AVO's gross negligence, willful misconduct, or its insurance obligations, AVO's total liability under this Agreement is limited to the amount Client paid AVO for the affected Vehicle's service in the prior thirty (30) days. Neither Party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages (including lost profits or loss of use).

8.3  Mutual indemnity. Each Party will indemnify the other against third-party claims arising from its own negligence or breach of this Agreement, to the extent of its fault.

9. Independent Contractor

9.1  AVO is an independent contractor. Nothing creates a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship. AVO controls the means and methods of the Services and is responsible for its personnel, taxes, and equipment.

10. Confidentiality

10.1  Each Party will keep the other's non-public business information (including pricing, routes, and customer data observed) confidential and use it only to perform this Agreement.

11. Termination

11.1  Either Party may terminate for material breach not cured within fifteen (15) days of written notice. Either Party may terminate for convenience on thirty (30) days' written notice. Client remains responsible for Services performed through the termination date.

12. Force Majeure

12.1  Neither Party is liable for delays or failures caused by events beyond its reasonable control (severe weather, acts of God, utility failure, government action). Outdoor Services may be rescheduled for weather (e.g., freezing temperatures, snow, hail).

13. Governing Law & Disputes

13.1  This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Colorado, without regard to conflict-of-laws rules. Venue for any dispute lies in the state courts located in Arapahoe County, Colorado. The Parties will attempt good-faith resolution, then non-binding mediation, before litigation. The prevailing Party in any dispute is entitled to its reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.

14. General

14.1  This Agreement, with its Exhibits, is the entire agreement and supersedes prior discussions. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties. If any provision is unenforceable, the rest remains in effect. Notices are effective when sent to the emails/addresses below. Neither Party may assign without the other's written consent, except to a successor of its business.

Exhibit B — Scope of Work

Tier 1

Maintenance / Light Recon

  • Exterior hand/foam wash & dry
  • Wheels, tires & wheel wells
  • All exterior glass
  • Interior trash removal
  • Interior vacuum (seats, floors)
  • Quick wipe of dash & console

Goal: keep the fleet consistently presentable on a tight schedule.

Tier 2

Standard Full Detail

  • Everything in Tier 1
  • Full interior vacuum & detail
  • Clean & dress all surfaces
  • Interior & exterior glass
  • Door jambs
  • Spray wax / sealant
  • Tire dressing

Goal: a thorough, customer-ready clean inside and out.

Tier 3

Deep Clean / Recon

  • Everything in Tier 2
  • Carpet/upholstery shampoo & hot-water extraction
  • Stain & odor treatment
  • Clay-bar decontamination
  • Hand wax / paint sealant
  • Plastic, vinyl & leather recondition

Goal: restore vehicles to like-new — for resale, lease-return, or brand reset.

This Exhibit is a checklist of included tasks; specific results depend on vehicle condition. Severely soiled vehicles may require a higher tier or add-ons, quoted before work begins. Rates effective June 2026 and aligned to AVO's published fleet detailing pricing.
