## What a "Tech Pack" Includes (so you know what you're paying for)

A tech pack is the blueprint a manufacturer needs to produce your garment consistently. A complete one contains:

- **Flat technical sketches** (front/back/detail views)
- **Bill of Materials (BOM)** — fabrics, trims, threads, hardware
- **Measurement / spec sheet** with tolerances, plus **size grading**
- **Colorways** and Pantone/color codes
- **Construction & stitching details**
- **Label, tag, and packaging specs**

If a quote doesn't list these, ask which are included — incomplete tech packs cause factory errors and re-do costs.
