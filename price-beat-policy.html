Price-Beat Policy · v1.0

We'll Beat Any Written Quote.

Hand this card to any customer comparing prices — or any crew member asked, "Can you really beat that?" Yes, here's how.

The Promise
AVO will beat any qualifying written competitor quote by 5%, down to our $89 minimum service charge.

How It Works

  1. Customer gets a written estimate from another licensed Denver-metro service company.
  2. Customer sends the estimate to AVO (email, photo, attached to the website quote form) before booking.
  3. AVO matches the scope of work, beats their price by 5%, and confirms the new price in writing.
  4. Customer approves — AVO gets to work.

What Qualifies

  • Written estimate (paper, PDF, email, screenshot) from a licensed and insured Denver-metro competitor
  • For a comparable scope of work — same service, same volume or area, same items
  • Dated within the last 30 days
  • Presented to AVO before booking
  • For services in AVO's standard area: Denver, Aurora, Boulder, & the Front Range

What's Excluded

  • Verbal quotes or "starting at" placeholder pricing
  • Aggregator auto-quotes (Thumbtack, Yelp instant quotes)
  • Quotes from unlicensed or uninsured providers
  • Promotional, coupon, or one-time pricing not generally available
  • Jobs AVO cannot accept liability for (hazardous materials, etc.)
  • Final price cannot go below AVO's $89 minimum service charge

Worked Example

Customer shows: a $400 written quote from a national chain for a half-trailer load.
AVO published rate for a half-trailer load: from $319.
Price-beat math: $400 minus 5% = $380.
Customer pays the lower of the two — in this case AVO's published $319 already wins, so the price-beat doesn't even need to kick in. (If the competitor quote had been below $319, AVO would beat it by 5% down to the $89 floor.)

For Crew On The Job

  • If a customer asks about price-matching, give them this card.
  • If they hand you a competitor quote on-site, photograph it and text it to dispatch before agreeing to a price. The 5% beat is calculated then; do not promise a number on the spot.
  • Add-on surcharges (heavy materials, excessive stairs, specialty disposal) are quoted separately and are not part of the price-beat calculation.
