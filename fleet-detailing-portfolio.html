Our Process

The AVO Multi-Point Recon Detail

Every vehicle runs the same disciplined checklist, then a final inspection under light before it's handed back. Nothing goes back on your line half-done.

Exterior

  • Foam pre-soak & safe two-bucket hand wash
  • Wheels, barrels & tire walls degreased
  • Door, hood & trunk jambs cleaned
  • Bug, tar & sap removal
  • Clay-bar paint decontamination*
  • Machine polish / swirl reduction*
  • Wax or spray sealant applied
  • Tires dressed, trim & plastics restored
  • Chrome & exhaust tips polished
  • Headlight clarity check / restoration*
  • Exterior glass cleaned, streak-free

Finishing & QC

  • Final walk-around inspection under light
  • Re-wipe of any missed touch points
  • Photo-ready check for your listing
  • Vehicle staged & keys returned

Interior

  • Full vacuum — seats, carpet, mats, trunk
  • Seat tracks, rails & crevices detailed
  • Vents, buttons & seams brushed out
  • Dash, console & doors cleaned + UV protectant
  • Leather cleaned & conditioned
  • Cloth seats & carpet shampooed / extracted*
  • Floor mats deep-cleaned
  • Cup holders & door pockets emptied & wiped
  • Headliner spot-cleaned
  • Interior glass & mirrors polished
  • Odor neutralized / ozone treatment*
  • All touch points sanitized & finger-print free

Our guarantee: if a unit isn't right, we re-do it free — before it ever goes back on your line.

* Included at the Full Recon / Premium Showroom level or as an add-on; standard at every level otherwise.
